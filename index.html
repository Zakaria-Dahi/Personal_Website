About me

I am a researcher with interest in the design and the use of classical and quantum artificial intelligence techniques for solving real-world issues. I am also a lecturer in computer science. My teaching include, quantum artificial intelligence, combinatorics and operations research, complex systems, object-oriented programming, databases and network and interconnection. I am a developer and I create AI-based solutions for smart cities (e.g. parking, etc.), economic applications, etc. I like to think of myself as somebody passionate about what he does. Never been afraid of not knowing something, maybe because it is my excuse to keep learning more. I admit, I like to keep that feeling of "nervous student studying hard the day before the exam" 🤓.
