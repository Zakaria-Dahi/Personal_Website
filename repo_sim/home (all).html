About the Repository: This repository regroups the existing quantum computer simulators. The classification is made according to a wide-enough set of criteria in order to guide your selection. This taxonomy is the result of our research work and analysis. So, if you have benefited somehow from our repository, please make sure to cite our work to knowledge our efforts. Also, please feel free to contact me if you want to contribute to enriching this list.
  • Plain citation: Zakaria Abdelmoiz Dahi and Enrique Alba and Rodrigo Gil-Merino and Francisco Chicano and and Gabriel Luque. A Survey on Quantum Computer Simulators. Proceedings of the 19th Conference of the Spanish Association for Artificial Intelligence, CAEPIA. 941-946

About Us: We are the NEO (Networking and Emerging Optimisation) research group located at the department of computer science (LCC) in the university of Malaga, Spain. We are a team of practitioners (senior distinguished, postdoctoral, and predoctoral researchers) that specialise in devising AI-assisted solutions for a large plethora of problems in real-life fields (e.g. smart cities, bio-informatics, communication networks, software engineering, etc.) using cutting edge technique based on artificial intelligence, distributed and quantum computing.
